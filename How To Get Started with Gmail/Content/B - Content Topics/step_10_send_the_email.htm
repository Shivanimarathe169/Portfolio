Step 10: Send the Email

Sending the email is the final step. Now that you have written your email body, added your recipients, signature, and a proper sign off, you are ready to send the email. Make sure that all details have been reviewed and are correct before clicking the "Send" button.

The Steps to Send an Email: (Refresher)

  1. Open Gmail and click on "Compose" to start a new email.

  2. Enter the recipient's email address in the "To" field.

  3. Add a subject in the "Subject" field to summarize the email content.

  4. Write your message in the email body.

  5. Attach files if needed by clicking the paperclip icon.

  6. Review the email for accuracy and completeness.

  7. Click the "Send" button to send your email.
