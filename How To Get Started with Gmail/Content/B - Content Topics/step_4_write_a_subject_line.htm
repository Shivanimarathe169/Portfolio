Step 4: Write a Subject Line

Writing a subject line is very important when drafting an email. Subject lines are a great way to capture reader's attention and summarize the purpose of the email. It is added to the "Subject" field through the email composition window.

Steps to Write a Subject Line:

  1. Open the "Compose" window.

  2. Add recipients.

  3. Type a clear, concise subject line in the "Subject" field.

    Example: "Request for Meeting Confirmation."

Pro Tip: Make sure that your subject line is crisp and short. The subject line should be short to read and convey your exact message.
