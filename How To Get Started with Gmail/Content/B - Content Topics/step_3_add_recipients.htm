Step 3: Add Recipients

Adding a recipient to the email means adding the receiver's email ID in the "To" input filed of the email composition window. There are three types of recipients you can add, "To, Cc, & Bcc".

An example of an email ID is - "christine@demoemail.com"

Steps to Add Recipients:

  1. Enter the recipient’s email address (ID) in the "To" field.

  2. Use the "Cc" field for recipients who should receive a visible copy of the email.

  3. Use the "Bcc" field for recipients whose email addresses should remain hidden.
