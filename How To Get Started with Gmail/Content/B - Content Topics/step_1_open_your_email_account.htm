Step 1: Open Your Email Account

If you have an existing email account, such as a Gmail account, open it and log in. If you do not have an existing account, sign up for one using the steps mentioned.

Steps to Sign Up for a Gmail Account:

  1. Visit the Gmail Website.

  2. Click "Create Account."

  3. Select the purpose of the account from the dropdown menu.

  4. Fill out the form with your personal data.

  5. Click "Next", then fill in the rest of the details and click on the confirmation button to create your account.

  6. Log in with your new credentials.
