Step 6: Insert a Sign-Off

A proper sign-off conveys professionalism and ensures the recipient understands the email is complete. It also leaves a positive impression, especially in formal or business settings.

Steps to Insert a Sign-Off:

  1. Compose an email via the "Compose" window

  2. Add a closing phrase, e.g., "Thank you," "Best regards," or "Warm regards."

  3. Optionally express gratitude or appreciation for the recipient’s time or efforts.
