Step 5: Structure the Body of the Email

When structuring the body of an email, consider these key elements:

Insert a Greeting:

  • Use a formal greeting, such as "Dear [Name]" or "To Whom It May Concern", for professional emails. For informal emails, casual greetings like "Hi" or "Hello" are appropriate.

Write the Content Body:

  • Opt for simple and clear language to ensure your message is easily understood.

  • Use active voice to make your writing more direct and engaging.

  • Format the text for readability by:

  • Including bullet points for lists.

  • Keeping paragraphs concise.
